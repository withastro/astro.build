On Tuesday, May 28th, 2024, the Astro core team hosted our first in-person meetup in Montreal, Canada. It was a pleasure to connect with the local developer community and share the latest Astro news. Thank you to everyone who travelled to meet us, support our keynote, and spend a lovely evening with us eating all the tacos, drinking all the coffee, pressing all the t-shirts, and indulging in all the cookies. We are proud to share a photo gallery of moments from the meetup as well as the full keynote presentation. 

**Post your photos on Twitter or Mastodon and tag us!** We'd love to see the moments you captured at the event and hear what your favourite memories of the night were.
