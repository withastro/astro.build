I am thrilled to announce [CloudCannon](https://cloudcannon.com/) as a new **official Astro partner**, including a new **$4,000 monthly donation** toward the ongoing open-source maintenance and development of Astro.

[CloudCannon](https://cloudcannon.com/) is a Git-based CMS that gives content teams the power to build and edit pages visually — without sacrificing the developer workflows you already love. CloudCannon works directly with your Git repository and local Markdown files, making it a natural complement to Astro's [content collections](https://docs.astro.build/en/guides/content-collections/). If you're looking for a CMS that embraces Astro's local-first approach to content, CloudCannon is a great choice.

## What This Partnership Means

CloudCannon first [added Astro support back in 2023](/blog/astro-cloudcannon-support/), and this new partnership deepens that relationship. Here's what we're working on together:

- **Deeper technical collaboration.** CloudCannon will get early access to new Astro features and work directly with our team to optimize their integration.
- **Better documentation.** We'll collaborate with CloudCannon on an official integration guide for the Astro community.

For the full story, check out our [livestream announcement](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qJXi2HdEanM) together with the CloudCannon team. To celebrate the launch, CloudCannon is also running the [Astro Challenge](https://cloudcannon.com/astro-challenge/) — build the best web page using their new [Astro Component Starter](https://cloudcannon.com/templates/astro-component-starter/) between March 12-19 for a chance to win $1,500.

## Astro ❤️ CloudCannon

We're grateful to the entire CloudCannon team for their support of Astro. This sponsorship funding will be invested directly into the open-source project to support the long-term maintenance of Astro, benefiting everyone. You can learn more about how we use these funds in our [FUNDING.md](https://github.com/withastro/.github/blob/main/FUNDING.md) on GitHub.

Thank you, CloudCannon!
