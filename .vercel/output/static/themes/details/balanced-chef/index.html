The Balanced Chef project! This open-source web application is built with Astro.js and Sanity to provide a comprehensive solution for meal planning and recipe management, helping users maintain a balanced and healthy diet effortlessly.

Balanced Chef simplifies the process of planning meals, discovering new recipes, and ensuring a wholesome culinary experience. With its intuitive user interface and powerful features, users can easily organize their recipes, plan meals for the week, and customize their meal plans based on dietary preferences.
